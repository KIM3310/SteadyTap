SteadyTap iOS accessibility-first

Calibration, adaptive challenge, and coaching for steadier touch interaction.

SteadyTap combines a native iOS app flow with an explicit FastAPI backend contract. This site is a free public landing page for the product story and reviewer path, not a replacement for the native experience.

Product flow

  • Calibration and drag profiling
  • Baseline and adaptive challenges
  • Before/after results and trend tracking
  • Optional cloud sync, coach-plan, and cohort benchmarks

Why this stays a landing page

  • The strongest proof is the iOS app running on device/simulator.
  • The backend contract matters, but the core experience is still native and human-centered.
  • A static public page keeps the repo legible without flattening it into a fake web app.
