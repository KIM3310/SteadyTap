SteadyTap iOS accessibility-first

Calibration, adaptive challenge, and coaching for steadier touch interaction.

SteadyTap is for people who need steadier touch, not just prettier charts. This page gives reviewers the shortest path into the real iOS interaction, the coaching loop, and the backend proof that supports it.

Product flow

  • Calibration and drag profiling
  • Baseline and adaptive challenges
  • Before/after results and trend tracking
  • Optional cloud sync, coach-plan, and cohort benchmarks

Why the proof stays native

  • The review-worthy interaction is the real tap flow on device or simulator.
  • The backend contract matters, but it supports the coaching loop instead of replacing it.
  • This page stays small on purpose so the repo reads like a product, not a browser clone.
