# Monetization Playbook - SteadyTap

This file is a commercial planning artifact, not a guarantee of revenue. Use it to make the repository legible as a sellable product, pilot, workshop, license, or proof-of-value package.

## Commercial Lane

- **Lane:** B2C/B2B2C accessibility coaching
- **Primary buyer:** Accessibility-focused users, care teams, educators, and mobile product teams.
- **Revenue wedge:** Offline-first iOS coaching with optional sync and caregiver/team reporting.

## Offer Ladder

1. **Freemium iOS app** - pricing hypothesis: Free + $4-$9/month individual
2. **Paid coaching plan templates** - pricing hypothesis: $99-$499/month small cohort
3. **Caregiver or education cohort dashboard pilot** - pricing hypothesis: $3k-$10k accessibility program pilot

## Acquisition Channels

- App Store landing page
- Accessibility educator partnerships
- Short demo of offline-first routine flow

## Proof Gates Before Selling

- Swift build passes
- Backend checks pass
- Local/offline mode is clear

## Risk Boundaries

- Sensitive routine data needs consent
- Avoid medical/therapeutic claims
- Sync must be opt-in

## 30/60/90 Day GTM

- **30 days:** Package 3 guided routines
- **60 days:** Create cohort-report mock
- **90 days:** Pilot with one educator/caregiver group

## Metrics That Matter

- Weekly active routines
- Routine completion
- Opt-in sync conversion

## Positioning Note

Lead with the specific operational pain, then show the runnable proof, then offer a narrow pilot. Do not pitch the repository as a vague app; pitch it as a concrete workflow with evidence, risk boundaries, and a next buying step.
